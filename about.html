Welcome To Shiva Associates


Shiva Associates is an Indian leading financial consulting firm, Authorised channel partner of multiple banks since 2008 based in Jaipur, Rajasthan. We deals in both secured and unsecured loans,  We work with clients to help them have a through look about their financial situations, make better decisions, get those decisions translated quickly into actions and sustain the momentum into the future.

Over last few years in the guidance of Mr. Raju Meena , we have worked passionately to bring the best services to clients. Our single discipline is putting client needs at the center of everything we do.

Shiva Associates offers a wide range of financial products and services to individuals and business owners. By seeking our sound financial information, we believe you will be better able to identify your goals and make sound decisions, to help you reach these goals.

We spend significant time in analysing client's business to provide best of financial assistance and out of the box approach in their profitable decision making. Our foremost focus always remains on how we can add value to our client's business.Our aim is to provide most efficient and cost effective services and offer our entire range of financial solutions in India and across the globe under one roof.
